Why TCP/IP?

TCP/IP is the foundation of all Internet communication. It ensures that data travels reliably, finds the right network, the right device, and even the right application running inside that device.

TCP/IP Layers

Application Layer
Transport Layer
Internet Layer
Network Access Layer

Application: User-facing apps like HTTP, FTP, email.

Transport: Reliability & session (TCP/UDP).

Internet: Logical addressing & routing (IP).

Network Access: Physical transmission (Ethernet/Wi-Fi).
